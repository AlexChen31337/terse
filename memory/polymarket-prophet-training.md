## 🚨 Red Flags I've Learned to Watch For

**Resolution Risk:**
- Ambiguous definitions ("military clash" vs "skirmish")
- Multiple interpretations possible
- No clear authoritative source
- New market creator (unproven track record)

**Liquidity Risk:**
- Wide bid-ask spreads (>10¢)
- Low 24h volume
- Few active traders
- Can't exit at displayed price

**Information Risk:**
- Insider activity possible (regulatory, corporate)
- Private information advantage
- Time-sensitive announcements
- Leaked data scenarios
