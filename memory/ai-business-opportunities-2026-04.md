## Executive Summary: AI Market Trends (Q1 2026)

### Global AI Market Size
- **2025:** ~$255B (Statista), growing to **$347B in 2026**
- **Projected 2030:** $1,218B+ (4.8x growth from 2025)
- **CAGR:** ~36-38% through 2030
- **Generative AI sub-market:** $63B (2025), growing at 42% CAGR

### Key Trends Defining 2026

1. **Agentic AI Goes Mainstream** — Autonomous AI agents are the #1 trend. The agentic AI market is projected to reach $8.5B by end of 2026 and $35-45B by 2030 (Deloitte). Multi-agent orchestration, not single agents, is where enterprise value lies. Anthropic's Claude Managed Agents, Microsoft's agentic tools, and open-source frameworks are competing for dominance.

2. **Inference Dominates Compute** — AI inference now accounts for 2/3 of all AI computing power (Deloitte TMT 2026). Data center buildout continues at $500B+ annually. Edge AI is growing but NOT replacing cloud — both are expanding simultaneously.

3. **AI Infrastructure as a Service** — AI-native infrastructure (GPU orchestration, inference optimization, model deployment) is a massive opportunity. Semiconductor supply chains remain fragile, making optimization and efficiency tools critical.

4. **Embedded AI > Standalone AI** — Deloitte predicts gen AI used within existing applications (search, SaaS, workflows) will be 300% more common than standalone tools. Integration/embedding is the distribution strategy.

5. **SaaS → Agent-as-a-Service** — Traditional seat-based SaaS pricing is shifting toward consumption- and outcome-based models as AI agents become the interface. Enterprises are redesigning workflows around agent capabilities.

6. **AI + Physical World (Robotics/Drones)** — Industrial robotics inflection point expected by 2030 (shipments doubling to 1M/year). AI-powered drones and autonomous systems for mining, agriculture, and logistics are accelerating.

7. **Technology Sovereignty** — Countries racing to build sovereign AI infrastructure. Australia is well-positioned with its Asia-Pacific data center corridor and strong regulatory environment.

8. **Vertical AI Applications** — Industry-specific AI solutions (finance, healthcare, mining, agriculture) command premium pricing and deeper moats than horizontal tools.

9. **AI Governance & Compliance** — With EU AI Act enforcement and US tariff impacts on AI hardware, compliance and governance tools are hot. The "fragmentation of AI toolchains" is a recognized pain point (MarketsandMarkets).

10. **Short-Form AI Content** — AI-generated micro-series/video is a $7.8B market in 2026. Content automation pipelines are highly monetizable.
