## Risk Management

### High Priority Risks

**1. Low Community Engagement**
- **Mitigation:** Direct outreach, bounties, gamification
- **Indicator:** <10 comments on issues by Feb 10
- **Action:** Escalate recruitment, offer higher bounties

**2. Architecture Disagreement**
- **Mitigation:** Clear voting process, tiebreaker (architect decides)
- **Indicator:** Split votes, no clear majority
- **Action:** Extended discussion, compromise proposal

**3. Technical Complexity**
- **Mitigation:** Phased approach, use Substrate templates
- **Indicator:** Development delays past Feb 28
- **Action:** Simplify scope, delay non-critical features

**4. Contributor Quality**
- **Mitigation:** Code review, CLA, contribution scoring
- **Indicator:** Low-quality PRs, spam
- **Action:** Stricter review, reject poor submissions
